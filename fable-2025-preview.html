Fable 2025 –
The RPG Reboot We've Been Waiting For

📅 March 2025✍️ Alex Storm⏱ 8 min read
📅 Release: 2025 — Xbox & PC

Overview

Fable is one of the most beloved RPG franchises in gaming history, and after more than a decade without a new entry, Playground Games — the studio behind the Forza Horizon series — is rebooting the franchise for modern hardware. The new Fable has been in development for several years and is one of Xbox's most anticipated exclusive titles.

Developer
Playground Games
Publisher
Xbox Game Studios
Platform
Xbox Series X/S & PC
Engine
ForzaTech (modified)

💡 Xbox Exclusive: Fable will be exclusive to Xbox Series X/S and PC (via Xbox Game Pass and Steam). PlayStation players will not have access to this game.

Setting and Story

The new Fable is set in Albion, the same beloved fantasy world of the original trilogy, but reimagined with modern graphics and expanded lore. The game appears to take place in a period that feels somewhat earlier than the original games, with a tone that balances the series' trademark British humor with more serious narrative stakes.

The reveal trailer starred Richard E. Grant as a mysterious villain and showcased a world of lush forests, quaint villages, and dark magical forces. The visual style blends the whimsical charm of classic Fable with stunning modern production values.

Gameplay Expectations

  • Return of the hero's moral choice system — good vs evil alignment
  • Body transformation based on playstyle and choices
  • Open world Albion exploration
  • Magic, melee, and ranged combat options
  • Property buying and town reputation systems returning
  • Day one on Xbox Game Pass
  • Co-op multiplayer confirmed
