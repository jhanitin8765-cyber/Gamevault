Dying Light: The Beast –
Kyle Crane Returns in 2025

📅 March 2025✍️ Nina Rao⏱ 6 min read
📅 Release: 2025

Overview

Dying Light: The Beast is Techland's exciting return to Kyle Crane — the protagonist of the original Dying Light — after years of experimentation with a new character in Dying Light 2. The Beast promises to combine the best elements of both games while introducing a dramatic new story that transforms Crane in unexpected ways.

💡 Big Change: Kyle Crane has been experimented on and now has superhuman abilities beyond normal parkour — including beast-like powers that change the gameplay significantly.

New Beast Powers

After being captured and subjected to horrific experiments, Kyle Crane has developed supernatural abilities that go far beyond the parkour skills of the original game. These beast-mode powers can be activated in combat, transforming the gameplay into something much more action-oriented when needed.

  • Beast mode — superhuman strength and speed for limited time
  • Enhanced parkour with wall-running and greater jump distance
  • New night mechanics — beast powers stronger at night
  • Improved crafting and weapon modification system
  • 4-player co-op returns and improved
  • Massive open world with day/night cycle
