South of Midnight –
Xbox's Most Unique New IP of 2025

📅 Release: 2025 — Xbox & PC

Overview

South of Midnight is one of the most visually distinctive new game announcements in recent years. Developed by Compulsion Games — the studio behind We Happy Few — this Xbox exclusive is a third-person action adventure set in a fantastical version of the American Deep South, drawing heavily from Southern Gothic folklore and mythology.

Setting and Story

You play as Hazel, a young woman from the American South who discovers she has magical weaving abilities after a catastrophic hurricane strikes her home. Using her powers, she must navigate a world filled with creatures from Southern folklore — enormous catfish monsters, bayou spirits, and other beings drawn from African American and Native American mythology.

  • Unique Southern Gothic setting unlike any other game
  • Stop-motion inspired visual style
  • Combat uses magical weaving abilities
  • Creatures drawn from authentic Southern folklore
  • Strong narrative focus on family and community
