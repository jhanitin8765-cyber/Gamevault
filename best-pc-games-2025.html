Top 10 Best PC Games of 2025 You Must Play

📅 March 2025✍️ GameVault Team⏱ 8 min read

2025 has been an extraordinary year for PC gaming. From massive open-world RPGs to innovative indie titles, the PC platform has delivered some of the best gaming experiences in recent memory. We have tested and reviewed dozens of games to bring you this definitive list of the top 10 PC games you absolutely must play right now.

#1

GTA VI

Rockstar's magnum opus redefines open-world gaming with its stunning Vice City recreation, dual protagonist story, and technical excellence. A generational gaming achievement that every PC gamer must experience.

Score: 9.5/10
#2

Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree

FromSoftware's masterpiece expansion adds 30+ hours of brutal, rewarding gameplay set in the haunting Land of Shadow. The best DLC ever made for one of the best games ever made.

Score: 9.8/10
#3

Baldur's Gate 3

Larian Studios created the definitive RPG experience with BG3. With thousands of choices, brilliant writing, and near-infinite replayability, this remains essential playing in 2025.

Score: 9.6/10
#4

Cyberpunk 2077: Phantom Liberty

After years of updates and patches, Cyberpunk 2077 is now one of the best games on PC. The Phantom Liberty expansion adds a gripping spy thriller storyline to an already excellent game.

Score: 9.2/10
#5

Alan Wake 2

Remedy Entertainment's surreal horror masterpiece pushes PC hardware to its absolute limits with stunning visuals and a mind-bending narrative that you won't forget easily.

Score: 9.0/10
#6

Helldivers 2

The best co-op shooter of the decade. Helldivers 2 delivers relentless, chaotic fun with friends in a game that just keeps getting better with every update.

Score: 8.8/10
#7

The Finals

The most innovative FPS in years, featuring fully destructible environments and team-based gameplay that creates unique moments every single match.

Score: 8.7/10
#8

Palworld

The gaming phenomenon of 2025 combines Pokemon-style creature collecting with survival crafting in an unexpectedly addictive package. Perfect for long gaming sessions.

Score: 8.5/10
#9

Hades II

Supergiant Games improves on one of the best roguelikes ever made with even more content, characters, and the same addictive "one more run" gameplay loop.

Score: 9.1/10
#10

Dragon's Dogma 2

Capcom's epic RPG sequel delivers an unparalleled sense of adventure with dynamic AI companions, massive monsters, and a living open world that reacts to your every decision.

Score: 8.9/10

Final Thoughts

2025 has proven that PC gaming is in its golden era. Whether you prefer massive open worlds, tight action games, or deep RPGs, this year has something extraordinary for every type of gamer. All ten of these games are highly recommended and represent the absolute best that PC gaming has to offer right now.
