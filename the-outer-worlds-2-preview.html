The Outer Worlds 2 –
Obsidian's Most Ambitious RPG Yet

📅 March 2025✍️ Alex Storm⏱ 7 min read
📅 Release: 2025 — Xbox & PC

Overview

The Outer Worlds 2 is the sequel to Obsidian Entertainment's acclaimed 2019 RPG. Building on the foundation of the original while addressing its criticisms, TOW2 promises to be a larger, deeper, and more ambitious sci-fi RPG experience. As an Xbox Game Studios exclusive, it is one of the most anticipated games for PC and Xbox players in 2025.

💡 Obsidian Pedigree: Obsidian Entertainment created Fallout: New Vegas, Pillars of Eternity, and the original Outer Worlds — some of the best RPGs ever made.

What Is Confirmed

  • New protagonist and new star system — fresh start for new players
  • Much larger world than the original game
  • Deeper companion system with more character development
  • Expanded dialogue and choice system
  • New faction system with greater complexity
  • Improved combat with more build variety
  • Stronger emphasis on player agency and consequence
  • Multiple ending paths based on decisions throughout

Improvements Over Original

The original Outer Worlds was praised for its writing, humor, and RPG mechanics but criticized for its relatively small world and lack of consequence for player choices. Obsidian has confirmed that TOW2 addresses all of these criticisms with a significantly larger game world, more meaningful choices, and consequences that ripple throughout the entire narrative.
